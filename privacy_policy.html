1. Introduction

FocusNest ("we", "our", or "the app") is an offline study toolkit that helps you plan tasks, use a focus timer, practice with flashcards and quizzes, and review mistakes. We respect your privacy and designed the app so your learning data stays on your device by default.

This Privacy Policy explains what information the app handles, how it is used, and what choices you have. By installing or using FocusNest, you agree to this policy.

2. Information we process

Data you create in the app (stored locally)

  • Study tasks (title, subject, duration, completion status, creation time)
  • Pomodoro session records (focus/break duration, completion time)
  • Flashcards and quiz results
  • Practice questions answers and mistake book entries
  • Formula library favorites and browsing (local content)
  • App settings and preferences

This data is saved in a local database on your device. We do not operate a user account system and do not upload this content to our servers in the current version of the app.

Data we do not collect

  • Name, email, phone number, or government ID
  • Precise location
  • Contacts, photos, microphone recordings, or SMS
  • Advertising identifiers for cross-app tracking (no ads SDK in the current version)

3. How we use information

Local data is used only to provide app features, including:

  • Displaying and editing your study plan and calendar view
  • Running the Pomodoro timer and showing session history
  • Flashcard practice, quizzes, and statistics
  • Practice mode, scoring, and mistake review
  • Local search across your saved content
  • Showing notifications when you enable reminders (see Permissions)

We do not sell your personal information.

4. Storage and retention

All study data is stored on your device until you delete it (for example by clearing app data, uninstalling the app, or using in-app delete actions where available). Uninstalling the app removes local app data from your device subject to your operating system behavior.

You are responsible for backing up your device if you wish to preserve data before uninstall or device reset. A future version may offer optional export/import; if added, this policy will be updated.

5. Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

Permission Purpose
Notifications (Android 13+) Optional Pomodoro completion reminders. You can deny or revoke this in system settings.
Vibration (if enabled by device) Optional alert when a focus session ends, depending on device settings.

We do not request access to the camera, microphone, location, or contacts for core features.

6. Third-party services

The current version of FocusNest does not include third-party analytics, advertising, or social login SDKs. Standard distribution through Google Play is subject to Google's own policies and processing as the store operator; please refer to Google Privacy Policy for details about Play services on your device.

7. Children's privacy

FocusNest is intended for general audiences including students. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 through our own servers. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us online, contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

8. Security

We use reasonable measures within the app design to keep data on your device. No method of electronic storage is 100% secure. Protect your device with a screen lock and keep your operating system updated.

9. International users

Because data is processed locally on your device, it generally does not leave your device through FocusNest itself. If you use the app while traveling, your data remains on the device unless you choose to back up or transfer it through other tools outside the app.

10. Your choices

  • Deny or revoke notification permission in Android settings
  • Delete tasks, mistakes, or other entries inside the app
  • Clear app storage or uninstall to remove all local app data

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the revised version at the same URL and update the "Last updated" date. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.
